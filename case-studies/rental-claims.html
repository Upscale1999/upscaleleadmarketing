The challenge

Legal services typically convert at 2 to 5% from lead to client, which makes paid acquisition difficult to justify. Most enquiries from broad-targeting ad campaigns are people with weak cases, no documentation or no intention of actually proceeding. To make the channel sustainable, the funnel had to do most of the qualification work before a lead ever reached a human.

Our approach

A Meta-led acquisition funnel with aggressive pre-qualification, fast follow-up and credibility built into every touchpoint.

  • Meta interest-based targeting for renters and recently-moved tenants, layered with demographic and location filters to reach people most likely to be dealing with deposit disputes.
  • Meta Lookalike Audiences modelled on previous successful claimants, high-precision targeting of people demographically and behaviourally similar to past paying clients.
  • Multi-touch Meta retargeting with credibility-building creative (testimonials, case results, regulatory trust signals) for visitors who hesitated on the first visit, legal services are a considered decision and rarely close on touch one.
  • Eligibility-based qualification built directly into the landing-page form: amount disputed, length of tenancy, deposit-protection-scheme status, and whether the tenant has documented evidence. People who don't qualify don't proceed, saving the team hours of dead-end calls every week.
  • Instant multi-channel follow-up across SMS, WhatsApp and email within minutes of submission. Legal-sector buyers respond to speed, and being first to make contact dramatically boosts conversion.
  • Credibility-led landing pages with case examples, success rates, "no win, no fee" reassurance and clear next-step expectations. Lowers buyer anxiety in a category where trust is everything.
  • Continuous A/B testing of landing-page headlines, qualification questions and ad creative, refining the funnel monthly based on which leads actually convert to paying clients, not just which generate volume.
The outcome

A consistent 20% lead-to-client conversion rate, four to ten times the legal-sector benchmark, turning paid Meta traffic into one of the firm's most predictable client acquisition channels. Ben can now plan campaign spend against expected client volume with confidence.
