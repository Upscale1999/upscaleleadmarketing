WHY IT WORKS

Most cold outreach fails. Here's why ours doesn't.

The difference between dead silence and a calendar full of meetings comes down to four things, and we get all four right.

Plain Text Beats HTML

HTML emails with images and tracking pixels get filtered straight to spam. We send plain, personal looking text, the kind your prospect actually reads. Higher inbox placement, higher reply rates.

AI Scale + Human Craft

AI personalises every email across thousands of prospects. Humans handle strategy, copy review and reply qualification. You get the volume of an SDR team without the headcount, and none of the spammy templated feel.

Deliverability First

Dedicated domains. Warmed mailboxes. SPF, DKIM, DMARC configured properly. Volume rotated across multiple inboxes. We obsess over deliverability because nothing else matters if your emails don't land in the inbox.

Before you ask

Common questions

Is this just cold email spam?

No. We send personalised, plain text emails at controlled volume from warmed mailboxes, targeting B2B decision makers with messages relevant to their role. Every prospect can opt out in one click. This is the opposite of spam, it's how the best modern sales teams prospect.

Will this affect my main domain or company email?

Never. We register and use dedicated sending domains (often lookalikes of your main domain) and provision separate mailboxes specifically for outreach. Your main domain and existing email reputation stay completely untouched.

How long until I see results?

Realistically, 6 to 8 weeks from kick off. Two to three of those weeks are warmup, non negotiable for deliverability. From week 4 you'll start seeing replies; meetings typically begin booking from week 6 to 8. Real pipeline impact is at 8 to 12 weeks once we've iterated on copy and scaled what works.

What happens when prospects reply?

We handle every reply for you. Positive replies get qualified and booked straight into your calendar. "Not now" replies go into a nurture sequence. Negative replies are removed. You only ever see the booked meetings, we deal with everything in between.

How do you handle data and compliance?

Securely. Outreach is targeted at B2B decision makers only, opt outs are honoured immediately, we document compliance throughout, and we never target consumers. Your prospect data and our processes are handled to a professional standard, and full documentation is available on request.

Can you target my specific industry?

If we can build a list of decision makers in your target market, we can run a campaign. We work across SaaS, professional services, agencies, recruitment, finance, manufacturing, healthcare, and more. On the discovery call we'll tell you straight whether we're a fit for your niche.

Do I have to sign a long contract?

We typically work on a 3 month minimum to give campaigns time to warm up and prove out, cold outreach won't work on a 30 day window. After that you're month to month. We'd rather earn the renewal than lock you in.
