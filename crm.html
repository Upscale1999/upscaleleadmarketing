Omnichannel messaging

Voice, SMS, WhatsApp and email. From one inbox.

Different leads reply on different channels. Some pick up the phone, some only respond to a text, some only check WhatsApp, some are email first. The UpScale CRM gives you one threaded inbox for all four, so your team never has to switch tabs, log into another app, or copy a conversation between platforms.

Voice calls flow through the CRM with automatic recording. SMS and WhatsApp arrive next to email in the same conversation thread. Every message is logged against the contact, so any team member can pick up where the last one left off.

  • Voice calls with automatic recording and transcription
  • SMS text messages, sent and received in-app
  • WhatsApp Business integration
  • Email, threaded per contact with two way sync
  • Templates, snippets and canned responses
  • Schedule sends, trigger by workflow, or reply live

Instant booking system

Customers book themselves in. And pay the deposit.

Manual scheduling is a bottleneck. The four emails to agree a time, the two follow ups when they ghost you, the missed slot when they don't show. The UpScale CRM's instant booking system removes all of it.

You embed a booking widget on your landing pages. Customers pick a time from your live calendar, answer the qualifying questions you set, and pay a deposit or booking fee upfront. It syncs to your calendar, sends the confirmation, and starts the reminder sequence. You wake up to a fully paid, pre qualified appointment.

  • Live calendar sync (Google, Outlook, iCal)
  • Take deposits or booking fees via Stripe or GoCardless
  • Qualifying questions before the booking is confirmed
  • Automatic SMS + email reminders to reduce no shows
  • Buffer times, availability windows, round robin routing
  • Rescheduling and cancellation flow built in

Frequently asked

Common questions about the CRM.

Is the CRM really included, or is it an upsell?

The core CRM plan is genuinely included with every UpScale lead generation campaign. That covers the contact database, sales pipeline, unified inbox for voice calls, SMS, WhatsApp and email, basic reporting and full data ownership. If you want the instant booking system with deposits, AI chatbots and auto-replies, call recording and compliance, advanced marketing automation or priority support, those unlock on paid upgrades. Pricing is transparent and there is no lock in contract.

What is in the core plan vs the paid upgrades?

Core plan (included with every campaign): customer database, kanban sales pipeline, unified inbox for voice calls, SMS, WhatsApp and email, basic reporting, and UK GDPR compliant data ownership.

Paid upgrades: instant booking with deposits, AI chatbots and auto-replies, call recording and compliance suite, advanced marketing automations, priority support, and additional resources or integrations. Pricing scales with what you actually use.

What communication channels does the CRM support?

Voice calls, SMS text messages, WhatsApp and email, all from a single inbox. Every conversation is threaded per contact, and outgoing messages can be scheduled or triggered by workflows. Voice calls are recorded automatically for compliance and coaching.

Do I own my customer data?

Yes. Every lead we deliver plus every contact you add is yours. Full export is available on request, and if you ever stop working with UpScale you keep your database. UK GDPR aligned throughout.

How does the AI chatbot work?

The AI reads inbound enquiries from every channel, answers the common questions using your own knowledge base, qualifies the lead against your ideal customer criteria, and only escalates hot ones to you. It runs 24/7 so you never miss an out of hours enquiry.

Can customers book straight into my calendar?

Yes. The booking system embeds on your landing pages and syncs to your calendar. You can require a deposit or booking fee upfront, ask qualifying questions, and set buffer times and availability windows per service.

Are call recordings compliant?

Yes. Every recorded call includes a compliant consent notice on connect. Recordings are stored securely, are searchable, and can be retained per your data policy. Useful for compliance audits, QA and sales coaching.
