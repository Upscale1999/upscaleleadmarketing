FAQ

UK lead generation, straight answers.

The questions UK business owners ask us most often on a first call.

Do you work with businesses outside Manchester?

Yes, the majority of our clients are outside Manchester. We've delivered campaigns for businesses in Northern Ireland, Scotland, Wales, and across England, from Plymouth to Newcastle. Delivery is remote-first via video calls, shared dashboards and WhatsApp, with in-person meetings when they actually help.

Can you run campaigns across multiple UK regions at once?

Yes. For multi-region or multi-branch businesses, we set up separate ad sets or campaigns by region, each with its own creative, landing page variants, audience targeting and reporting view. You see what's working in each region, not a blended national average that hides the detail.

What's the typical UK cost per lead by sector?

Rough national benchmarks: home services £30 to £80 per lead, healthcare and cosmetic £40 to £120, legal £50 to £150, automotive £20 to £60, B2B meetings £100 to £350 per booked call. London consistently runs 30 to 60 percent above these. We optimise to cost-per-acquisition (lead-to-customer), not cost-per-lead, because that's what actually pays the bills.

Can you target rural or low-population areas?

Yes. For rural service businesses with broader catchments (often 30 to 50 mile radii), we adjust audience size, creative angle and bidding strategy accordingly. Smaller audiences need longer learning periods and different creative rotation, but the system absolutely works outside major cities.

Do you do Ireland too?

Yes, we work with clients in both Northern Ireland and the Republic of Ireland. Northern Motor Group, one of our case studies, is based in Northern Ireland, and we run campaigns into the Republic for several B2B clients. EU GDPR and ROI ad market differences are baked into how we run those accounts.

Are your leads exclusive even on UK-wide campaigns?

Yes. Every lead goes only to the client whose campaign generated it. We don't operate a lead-sharing model. If we sign you nationally for a sector, we won't take a second national competitor, regional yes, national no.
